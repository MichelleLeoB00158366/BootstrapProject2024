Welcome to Happy Clothes

At Happy Clothes, we make fashion recycling easy and profitable. Send us your clothes in bags, get paid per kilogram, and let us handle the rest. We expertly select, wash, and prepare your items for resale, offering them individually or through our exclusive monthly plans. Discover a new way to refresh your wardrobe and make a positive impact on the environment.

Who are we?

At Happy Clothes, we’re dedicated to making fashion accessible, sustainable, and impactful. We believe that looking good shouldn’t come at the cost of the planet or your wallet.

Eco-Friendly Practices

Our recycling and upcycling efforts help reduce textile waste while promoting a greener, more sustainable world.

Driving Positive Change

We’re reshaping the fashion industry through our innovative resale model that creates meaningful social impact.

Affordable Style

We provide stylish, high-quality clothing options for everyone through accessible monthly plans and individual selections.

We Lead by Example

Our commitment to sustainability, affordability, and innovation drives everything we do. We don’t just follow trends—we set them, with our eyes focused on creating a better future for fashion and the environment.
