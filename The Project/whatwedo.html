Who is behind Happy Clothes ?

At Happy Clothes, we make fashion recycling easy and profitable. Send us your clothes in bags, get paid per kilogram, and let us handle the rest. We expertly select, wash, and prepare your items for resale, offering them individually or through our exclusive monthly plans. Discover a new way to refresh your wardrobe and make a positive impact on the environment.

What We Do

Our mission is simple: helping families afford quality clothes while promoting sustainability.

I am Michelle Leone, a mother of three girls, aged 3, 5, and 7. Like many parents, I face the challenges of inflation and the rising cost of living. To make ends meet, I came up with the idea of buying used clothes from other parents, paying them per kilogram, and ensuring each item is cleaned and ready for resale. This allows families to save money while reducing waste and making fashion more sustainable.

We provide a range of clothing options, from sports gear and wedding outfits to hypoallergenic cotton clothes. Our subscription boxes make it even easier for families to stay within budget and keep their kids stylish. By simply entering your child's age, height, and preferences, we deliver a box with either 2, 4, or 6 items each month.
